Printable Flash Cards

A simple website for printing approximately credit card sized flash cards onto A4.






Important Printing Note

This is designed to print 12 cards per A4 paper set to landscape (it will take as many words as you have but 12 per sheet).

Make sure in the print settings the scale is set to 100 and the margins are set to 'none'. The cards should completely cover the page.

Tested with Firefox and Chrome Nov 2021 - other browsers may or may not work.
